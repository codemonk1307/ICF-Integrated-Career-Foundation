About Us

Welcome to ICF (Integrated Career Foundation), where we cultivate the future leaders of the accounting profession. We are dedicated to providing top-tier training and placement support for individuals aspiring to excel in the field of accounting.

At ICF, we recognize that a strong foundation is essential for success in the dynamic world of finance. Our comprehensive curriculum is meticulously crafted to encompass both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, ensuring that our students are equipped with the expertise demanded by today's industry standards.

What sets ICF apart is our unwavering commitment to our students' success beyond the classroom. Our integrated approach includes personalized career guidance and placement support services, aimed at connecting our graduates with lucrative employment opportunities in reputable firms.

Our Courses On Computer Accounting (CCA)

Computer Accounting (CCA) - Basic

Duration: 6 Months

A career-oriented foundation course designed to equip students with core accounting, taxation, and office management skills. Ideal for freshers aiming to enter the finance and accounting sector.

Basic Accounting

Understand the core principles of accounting including double-entry system, journal entries, ledgers, trial balance, and preparation of final accounts. Builds a strong base for advanced financial concepts.

Build your financial foundation
MS Office

Gain practical knowledge of MS Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. Learn to create professional documents, spreadsheets for data handling, and impactful presentations for office tasks.

Master the tools of the modern office
Tally Prime

Get hands-on training in Tally Prime, one of the most widely used accounting software. Learn to manage day-to-day accounting, inventory tracking, billing, and generate essential financial reports.

Smart accounting starts here
Goods & Services Tax (GST)

Learn the structure and application of GST in business. Understand registration, tax calculation, invoice preparation, and filing of GST returns using Tally.

Understand GST, power your compliance
TDS (Tax Deducted at Source)

Understand the concept of TDS, applicable rates, and deduction process. Get trained in preparing TDS returns and compliance through practical examples and software use.

Learn to deduct, file, and comply
Business Communication

Enhance your communication skills for a corporate environment. Learn to write professional emails, business letters, reports, and improve your verbal communication for interviews and meetings.

Speak smart. Write right
Computer Accounting (CCA) - Advanced

A complete and career-focused program designed for students who want to build strong skills in accounting, taxation, and financial software. Ideal for those looking to work in finance or business operations.

Duration: 12 Months

Basic Accounting

Learn the core principles of accounting, including journal entries, ledgers, trial balance, and financial statements. Build a strong foundation in how money moves in a business and how records are maintained properly.

Master the language of business
MS Office

Get hands-on training in MS Excel, Word, and PowerPoint. Learn advanced Excel tools like formulas, charts, and pivot tables, plus how to create professional reports, documents, and presentations used in business.

Work smarter with pro-level tools
Tally Prime

Learn how to use Tally Prime for managing day-to-day business transactions. Covers GST entries, payroll, inventory, and other advanced features used by accountants in real companies.

Advanced accounting. Real results
Goods & Services Tax (GST)

Understand the full GST system, from basic rules to return filing. Learn how to apply GST in real situations using Tally, and how to manage invoices, reconciliation, and compliance for businesses.

Master GST. Manage business compliance
Direct Tax (Including TDS)

Gain knowledge of Income Tax rules, deductions, and how to file returns for individuals and businesses. Also includes TDS rules, compliance steps, and practical training on tax-related software.

Navigate taxation like a pro
Business Communication

Improve your speaking, writing, and presentation skills for the workplace. Learn how to write emails, speak in meetings, build a good resume, and prepare for job interviews with confidence.

Communicate with clarity and confidence
Busy Accounting Software

Learn to use Busy software for managing GST billing, ledger entries, inventory, and reports. This software is popular among small and medium businesses for easy and reliable accounting.

Versatility in accounting at your fingertips
SAP (FICO) – S/4HANA

Understand how large companies use SAP for financial operations. Learn the basics of SAP FICO including account management, cost tracking, and budgeting using the S/4HANA system.

Step into enterprise-level finance
Power BI

Learn how to use Power BI to convert raw data into clear reports and dashboards. Build skills in data analysis, visualization, and storytelling that help in financial decision-making and reporting.

Communicate with clarity and confidence
